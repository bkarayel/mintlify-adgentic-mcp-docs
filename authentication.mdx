<Card
  title="1) Sign up on Adgentic Publisher Platform"
  icon="arrow-up-right-from-square"
  href="https://publisher.adgenticplatform.com/"
  arrow="true"
>
  Create an account to access the dashboard.
</Card>

<Card
  title="2) Obtain your ADGENTIC_API_KEY"
  icon="arrow-up-right-from-square"
  href="https://publisher.adgenticplatform.com/access/api-keys/"
  arrow="true"
>
  Go to <em>API Keys</em> tab in the publisher platform and create a new key.
</Card>

<Card title="3) Provide your ADGENTIC_API_KEY either:">
  <ul>
    <li>
      Per call:
      <code>api_key: &lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;</code>
    </li>
    <li>
      Or as an environment variable:
      <Tabs>
        <Tab title="macOS / Linux (bash, zsh)">
          <CodeBlock language="bash">export ADGENTIC_API_KEY=&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;</CodeBlock>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Windows (CMD)">
          <CodeBlock language="bash">set ADGENTIC_API_KEY=&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;</CodeBlock>
        </Tab>
        <Tab title="Windows (PowerShell)">
          <CodeBlock language="bash">$Env:ADGENTIC_API_KEY=&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;</CodeBlock>
        </Tab>
      </Tabs>
    </li>
  </ul>
</Card>
