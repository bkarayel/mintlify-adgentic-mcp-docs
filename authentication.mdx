<Card
  title="1) Sign up on Adgentic Publisher Platform"
  icon="arrow-up-right-from-square"
  href="https://publisher.adgenticplatform.com/"
  arrow="true"
>
  Create an account to access the dashboard.
</Card>

<Card
  title="2) Obtain your ADGENTIC_API_KEY"
  icon="arrow-up-right-from-square"
  href="https://publisher.adgenticplatform.com/"
  arrow="true"
>
  Go to <em>API Keys</em> in the dashboard and create a new key.
</Card>

<Card title="3) Provide your ADGENTIC_API_KEY either:">
  <ul>
    <li>Per call: <code>"api_key": "&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;"</code></li>
    <li>
      Or as an environment variable:
      <p><strong>macOS / Linux (bash, zsh)</strong></p>
      <pre><code>export ADGENTIC_API_KEY="&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;"</code></pre>

      <p><strong>Windows (CMD)</strong></p>
      <pre><code>set ADGENTIC_API_KEY="&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;"</code></pre>

      <p><strong>Windows (PowerShell)</strong></p>
      <pre><code>$Env:ADGENTIC_API_KEY="&lt;YOUR_KEY&gt;"</code></pre>
    </li>
  </ul>
</Card>
