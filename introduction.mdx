Adgentic makes it effortless for AI applications to monetize with its commerce search engine and built-in monetization layer. Our platform is directly integrated with the world’s leading affiliate networks, giving developers access to thousands of advertisers and millions of brand-safe products and services.


Through the Adgentic MCP Server and APIs, you can instantly match user prompts with real-time commercial offers. We handle the heavy lifting from product discovery to commission payouts so you can focus on building great AI experiences.


Adgentic provides a unique tracking link with every product response so when your users complete a purchase, you earn commissions. Behind the scenes, we work directly with advertisers to negotiate the highest commission rates, maximizing your revenue potential.
